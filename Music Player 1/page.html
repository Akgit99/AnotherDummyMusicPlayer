Coldplay
British Band

Coldplay are a British rock band formed in London in 1997. They consist of vocalist and pianist Chris Martin, guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist Guy Berryman, drummer Will Champion and creative director Phil Harvey. They met at University College London, initially calling themselves Big Fat Noises and changing to Starfish, before the current name.
